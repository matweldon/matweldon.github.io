I am currently working as a Teaching Associate in the Department of Economics at Lancaster University. Before that, I have worked as a Research Associate at Lancaster University and at the University of Stirling.

My research focuses on topics in the economics of education, decision-making, and structural empirical work based on models of economic matchings (such as allocations of pupils to schools).
